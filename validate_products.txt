VALIDATION NOTES (Feb 21, 2026)

Product 1: Liniment Oléo-Calcaire
- US search would show: very limited presence, mostly French expat blogs
- Manufacturing: Simple emulsion, any cosmetic co-packer can make (oil + lime water)
- MOQ likely under 500 units
- US gap: HUGE - American parents don't know this exists
- Twist: Position as "French method" no-wipe diaper care, sensitive skin angle
- Target: Crunchy moms, cloth diaper users, babies with eczema
- Estimated COGS: $3-4 per 8oz bottle, retail $18-24
- Go-to-market: Amazon FBA + mom Facebook groups + TikTok demos

Product 2: Savon Noir (Black Soap Body Polish)
- US search would show: Some hammam imports on Amazon, poorly branded
- Strong import presence exists but no premium US brand owns the category  
- Manufacturing: Olive oil + potassium hydroxide, can source from Morocco or make domestic
- US gap: Moderate - Turkish/Moroccan shops sell it, but no DTC brand
- Twist: Instagram spa-at-home ritual kit with kessa glove, beautiful packaging
- Target: Skincare enthusiasts, spa lovers, people who buy Herbivore/Drunk Elephant
- Estimated COGS: $5-7 per jar, retail $28-35
- Go-to-market: Shopify DTC, TikTok "spa day" content, Sephora wholesale later

Product 3: Huile de Douche (Shower Oil)  
- US search would show: L'Occitane and Weleda exist but positioned as luxury/niche
- Market gap: NO mass-market option, everyone still buys drying body wash
- Manufacturing: Oil blend (sweet almond/sunflower + emulsifier), easy to formulate
- US gap: Large - Americans don't understand shower oil category
- Twist: "The body wash for people who hate dry skin" - education-heavy marketing
- Target: People with eczema, dry climate dwellers, winter skincare  
- Estimated COGS: $4-6 per 8oz, retail $16-22
- Go-to-market: Amazon + "Why your body wash is drying you out" content marketing

Product 4: Medical-Grade Lip Balm Stick
- US search would show: Aquaphor, Cerave, but mostly tubs/tubes not sticks
- Limited stick format for serious repair (most are just waxy gloss)
- Manufacturing: Standard lip balm production, need good formula (lanolin/bisabolol/centella)
- US gap: Moderate - people buy Aquaphor tubs but want stick convenience  
- Twist: "For Accutane users and mountain athletes" - medical positioning
- Target: Accutane patients, climbers, skiers, chronic chapped lip sufferers
- Estimated COGS: $1.50-2 per stick, retail $8-12
- Go-to-market: Amazon FBA, partner with dermatologists, Reddit skincare communities

Product 5: Fast-Dry Microfiber Gym Towel  
- US search would show: Tons of cheap microfiber towels on Amazon
- VERY crowded market, hard to differentiate
- Manufacturing: Commodity item, thousands of Alibaba suppliers
- US gap: None - saturated category
- REJECT: Too competitive, no defensible twist
