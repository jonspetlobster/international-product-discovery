# Brand Revalidation Batch 1 Report

**Date:** 2026-03-01  
**Batch:** 1/10 (Ideas 1-10)  
**Validation:** Amazon, Target, Google Shopping, TikTok competitive research

## Summary

**Total Reviewed:** 10 ideas

**Average Revalidated Score:** 7.2/10  
**Major Downgrades:** 3 ideas (idea_033, idea_013, idea_034)

   - ✅ Simple manufacturing

   - ⚠️ Peanut allergies limit market
   - ⚠️ Shelf life concerns (oil in ground peanuts)

3. **idea_037 - GhribaCo (Moroccan Cookies)** - **7.9/10** (was 8.71)

## Key Insights

### Scoring Reality Check
- **Original scores were inflated** - avg 8.6 reflects wishful thinking
- **Harsh revalidation avg 7.2** - closer to reality but still generous
- **True avg should be ~5.0** with 60%+ rejection rate for realistic assessment

### Competition Underestimated
- **3 ideas had MAJOR competitive overlaps** not identified in original research
- Post-COVID categories (hand sanitizer, home products) are saturated
- DTC brands move fast - gaps close quickly

### Manufacturing Simplicity ≠ Success
- Simple products (cinnamon sugar, mayo sauce) already exist
- "Unique positioning" doesn't overcome commodity competition
- Need defensible differentiation beyond packaging

## Recommendations

1. **Prioritize ideas 010, 002, 037** - genuine gaps with clear differentiation
2. **Proceed cautiously with 001, 016, 022, 032** - viable but crowded
3. **De-prioritize 013, 033** - competitive overlaps reduce opportunity
4. **Reject 034** - direct competitor exists
