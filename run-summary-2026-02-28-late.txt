### 1. ScrubCraft - Artisan Cleaning Paste
**Score: 8.3/10**

- Format change: DIY method → ready-to-use product

## Key Insights

**Dutch Cleaning Philosophy:**
- Minimalism: One product for many uses (allesreiniger)
- Concentration: Buy concentrate, dilute yourself (80% vinegar)
- Specialization: HG has 300+ products for specific surfaces/problems
- Value: Dishwasher powder > pods, bulk > individual packaging
- Eco-friendly: Biodegradable, unscented, natural options mainstream

**US Market Gaps:**
1. Pre-made convenience versions of DIY cleaning methods (baking soda paste, vinegar mixes)
2. Educational kits teaching safe cleaning chemistry
3. Bridge between commercial/janitorial and consumer products
4. Premium positioning for basic cleaning concepts

**Manufacturing Notes:**
- Paste products need shelf-life testing (60+ days)
- Pre-measured packets require co-packer with pouch filling capability
- Private label options exist for most cleaning formulas
- Glass jars add premium feel but increase shipping costs
